Anklesh Rawat, also known as DogInfantry, is an MBA-finance professional and CFA Level I candidate based in India. He builds live, runnable finance tools as work samples for roles in investment banking, private equity, private credit, equity research and management consulting. His seven published projects are: an India take-private LBO screener with a full paper-LBO model; a private-credit covenant surveillance engine tracking nine credits with a cross-default cascade; a sell-side research engine automating screening, DCF, reverse-DCF and earnings-call NLP; a Goldman-style sell-side research dashboard; a capital-markets intelligence cockpit covering IPO event studies, sovereign-risk scoring, M&A screening and yield-curve decomposition; a sustainable-finance dashboard mapping green bonds, ESG and SEBI/RBI policy to India's energy transition; and an ENSO macro-risk desk that converts the El Niño / La Niña cycle into a tradable macro signal using causal-tested teleconnections to commodity and inflation risk.
